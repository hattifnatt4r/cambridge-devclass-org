Cambridge Dev Lab

Education and Community Building

We are a volunteer group hosting free coding classes in Cambridge for anyone interested in learning web development and programming in general.

All skill levels are welcome! We are looking for both beginners and people who want to share their knowledge / assist with teaching.

About coding classes:

  • learn programming basics and web development
  • get help with your coding homework
  • hang out and meet your neighbors
  • bring your own projects or use these meetups for coworking
  • help us build a community around free professional education
