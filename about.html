About us

We are a volunteer group hosting free coding classes and other community events in Cambridge, MA.

The project began in October 2024 with small coding classes for 1–3 students in the common area of the Cambridge Public Library. By mid-2025, our meetups had grown to around 15 attendees with the help of regular volunteers. We expanded beyond coding to include community events such as board game nights. In 2026, we are partnering with the Cambridge Public Library to host a series of "Coding and Web Development 101" workshops.
